[PHTITLE]USAGE OF 'CABER'


General uses of 'caber':

	to fit, to be room for:
		Cabe más.  (There's room for more. / More can fit.)

	to be fitting or possible:
		 No cabe duda que está triste.  (Undoubtedly he's sad.)
		Cabe que puedo estar aquí.  (It's fitting that I can be here.)


caber en ... :
	to fit in something:
		El autobús cabe en el garaje.  (The bus fits in the garage.)


caber por ... :
	to fit through something:
		El barco no cabe por el canal.  (The boat is too big to pass through the canal.)


[número 1] entre [número 2] cabe a [número 3]:
 	[number 1] divided by [number 2] equals [number 3]:
		9 entre 3 cabe a 3    (9 divided by 3 is 3)
