[PHTITLE]USAGE OF 'BUSCAR'


buscar [something]:
	to look for something:
		Busco mi lápiz.  (I am looking for my pencil.)


buscar a [someone]:
	to look for someone:
		Buscamos a Pedro.  (We are looking for Peter.)


buscar a [someone]:
	to pick someone up:
		Buscaré a los niños a las dos de la tarde.  (I'll pick up the children at 2 p.m.)


se busca [name/item]:
	 [noun] wanted:
		Se busca cocinero.  (Cook wanted.)
		Se busca computadora. (Computer wanted.)


buscársela:
	to look for trouble / to find trouble:
		Ella se la buscó en las calles.  (She found trouble on the streets.)
		Ellos se la buscaban.  (They were looking for trouble.)
