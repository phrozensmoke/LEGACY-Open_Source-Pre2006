[PHTITLE]PRETERIT PERFECT TENSE

[PH-HI]The Preterit Perfect tense is rarely used in everyday spanish, and, therefore does not warrant a lengthy discussion.  However, a sample conjugation of this tense appears below for your informational purposes:[/PH-HI]

     Yo hube hablado.      [I had spoken.]
     Tú hubiste hablado.      [You had spoken.]
     Ella / Él / Usted hubo hablado.      [She had spoken.]
     Nosotros hubimos hablado.      [We had spoken.]
     Vosotros hubisteis hablado.      [You all had spoken.]
     Ellos / Ustedes hubieron hablado.      [They had spoken.]
