[PHTITLE]USES OF 'TENER'

[PHSECTION]General 'tener' uses: 
	[PH-HI]to be ... years old:[/PH-HI]    tener ... años :

	Yo tengo 18 años.  (I am 18 years old.) 
	Ramon tenía 27 años cuando ...   (Ramon was 27 years old when ...)
                                                                                                         
                                              
	[PH-HI]to be (very) hungry[/PH-HI] -  tener (mucha) hambre 
	[PH-HI]to be (very) thirsty[/PH-HI] -   tener (mucha) sed 
	[PH-HI]to be afraid (of) [/PH-HI] -  tener miedo (de) 
	[PH-HI]to be (very) cold[/PH-HI] -  tener (mucho) frío 
	[PH-HI]to be (very) hot[/PH-HI]  -  tener (mucho) calor 
	[PH-HI]to be right (wrong)[/PH-HI]  -  (no) tener razon 
	[PH-HI]to be sleepy[/PH-HI] -   tener sueño 
	[PH-HI]to be in a hurry[/PH-HI]  -  tener prisa 
	[PH-HI]to have to do something[/PH-HI] -  tener + 'que'+ infinitive
                                                       

[PHSECTION]Temperature with 'tener': 

	[PH-HI]I am hot.[/PH-HI] -  Tengo calor.
	[PH-HI]This (something) is hot.[/PH-HI] -   Està caliente.
	[PH-HI]It is hot (of weather).[/PH-HI] -  Hace calor.
