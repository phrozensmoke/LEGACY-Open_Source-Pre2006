[PHTITLE]ACCENTS TO REMEMBER


Certain words change their meaning when written with an accent:

cómo: how
como: like

dé: give (command)
de: of, from

él: he/him
el: the

más: more
mas: but

mí: me
mi: my

sé: I know
se: 3rd person pronoun

sí: yes
si: if

sólo: only (adverb)
solo: alone

té: tea
te: you

tú: you
tu: your
