[PHTITLE]OTHER FUTURE TENSES

One can use the future perfect, the future progressive, and the future perfect progressive to make certain emphasis in meaning beyond what the simple future tense conveys. For example:

[PH-HI]Simple Future:[/PH-HI]
	Yo veré.   (I will look.)  - Sometime, for sometime.
	* See the "Future Tense" help file for more information.


[PHSECTION]FUTURE PROGRESSIVE:                                                                  
	Yo estaré viendo.  (I will be looking.)  -  Continued process

[PH-HI]To form the Future Progressive tense, use the simple future tense of 'estar' + a present participle:[/PH-HI] 
          estaré / estarás / estará / estaremos / estaréis / estarán    + present participle

     Yo estaré caminando      [I will be walking.]
     Tú estarás caminando      [You will be walking.]
     Ella / Él / Usted estará caminando      [She will be walking.]
     Nosotros estaremos caminando      [We will be walking.]
     Vosotros estaréis caminando      [You all will be walking.]
     Ellos / Ustedes estarán caminando      [They will be walking.] 



[PHSECTION]FUTURE PERFECT:                                                                    
	Yo habré visto.  (I will have looked (by the time...))

[PH-HI]To form the Future Perfect tense, use the simple future tense of 'haber' and a past participle:[/PH-HI] 
          habré / habrás / habrá / habremos / habréis / habrán   + past participle  

     Yo habré caminado      [I will have walked.]
     Tú habrás caminado      [You will have walked.]
     Ella / Él / Usted habrá caminado      [She will have walked.]
     Nosotros habremos caminado      [We will have walked.]
     Vosotros habréis caminado      [You all will have walked.]
     Ellos / Ustedes habrán caminado      [They will have walked.]



[PHSECTION]FUTURE PERFECT PROGRESSIVE:                                                                    
	Yo habré estado viendo.  (I will have been looking. (by the time...))  -  Continued process

[PH-HI]To form the Future Perfect Progressive tense, use the simple future tense of 'haber' + 'estado' + a present participle:[/PH-HI] 
          habré / habrás / habrá / habremos / habréis / habrán   + 'estado' + present participle

     Yo habré estado caminando      [I will have been walking.]
     Tú habrás estado caminando      [You will have been walking.]
     Ella / Él / Usted habrá estado caminando      [She will have been walking.]
     Nosotros habremos estado caminando      [We will have been walking.]
     Vosotros habréis estado caminando      [You all will have been walking.]
     Ellos / Ustedes habrán estado caminando      [They will have been walking.]
