[PHTITLE]USEFUL VERB CONSTRUCTIONS


[PH-HI]To begin to do something.:[/PH-HI] 
	'Emepezar/Comenzar' +'a'+ infinitive: 
		Uds. empiezan a hablar muy bien el español. (You're beginning to speak spanish very well.)
		Comienzo a besarte.  (I begin to kiss you.)

                               
[PH-HI]To be going to do something.:[/PH-HI] 
	'Ir' +'a'+ infinitve: 
		Voy a ir de compras esta tarde. (I am going to go shopping this afternoon.) 

                                                                   
[PH-HI]To continue doing something.:[/PH-HI] 
	'Continuar' +'a'+ gerundio: 
		Continuo estudiando el español. (I am continuing studying spanish.) 

                               
[PH-HI]To do something again.:[/PH-HI] 
	'Volver' +'a'+infinitive: 
		¿Cuándo vuelves a jugar al tenis?  (When are you going to play tennis again?) 

                                                                   
[PH-HI]To just finish doing something.:[/PH-HI] 
	'Acabar' +'de+' infinitive: 
		Acabo de escribir un tarjeta. (I just finished writing a postcard.)


[PH-HI]To play sport.:[/PH-HI] 
	'Jugar'+'al'+ noun: 
		Juego al tenis.  (I play tennis.)
		NOTE: Some Spanish speakers omit 'al'.


[PH-HI]To plan, intend to do something.:[/PH-HI] 
	'Pensar'+ infinitive: 
		¿Cuándo piensas contestar la carta?   (When do you plan to answer the letter?) 

                               
[PH-HI]To think about something.: [/PH-HI]
	'Pensar' +'en': 
		¿En qué piensas?   (What are you thinking about?)
