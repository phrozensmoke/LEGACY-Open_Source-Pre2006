[PHTITLE]USAGE OF 'GUSTAR'

[me/te/le/nos/les/os] gustar [something or infinitive]
	[PH-HI]someone likes something:[/PH-HI]
		Me gusta el libro.  (I like the book. [Literally, the book pleases me.]
		Me gusta viajar.  (I like to travel.)

[me/te/le/nos/les/os] gustar [something] a  [someone]:
	[PH-HI]someone likes something:[/PH-HI]
		Le gustan los libros a Ana.   (Ana likes the books.)

[me/te/le/nos/les/os] gustar de  [something]:
	[PH-HI]someone enjoys something:[/PH-HI]
		Me gusta de las playas.  (I enjoy the beaches.)

gustar [something]:
	[PH-HI]to taste something:[/PH-HI]
		No he gustado el pan.   (I have not tasted the bread.)

gustar:
	[PH-HI]to please:[/PH-HI]
		La película gusta.  (The film is pleasing.)

¿Usted gusta?  or  ¿Gusta?:
	Would you like some?

Como usted gusta.  or   Como  gustas.:
	As you wish.
