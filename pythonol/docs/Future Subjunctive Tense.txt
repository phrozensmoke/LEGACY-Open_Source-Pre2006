[PHTITLE]FUTURE SUBJUNCTIVE TENSE

The present subjunctive has taken over most functions of the future subjunctive; therefore, the future subjunctive is not often used, but you may see it in literature.

     Yo hablare      [I will possibly speak.]
     Tú hablares      [You will possibly speak.]
     Ella / Él / Usted hablare      [She will possibly speak.]
     Nosotros habláremos      [We will possibly speak.]
     Vosotros hablareis      [You all will possibly speak.]
     Ellos / Ustedes hablaren      [They will possibly speak.]
