[PHTITLE]USAGE OF 'PENSAR'

pensar:
	[PH-HI]to think:[/PH-HI]
		Mi hermano no piensa.   (My brother doesn't think.)


pensar en [something or someone]:
	[PH-HI]to think about [something or someone]:[/PH-HI]
		Pensamos en tí.  (We're thinking about you.)


pensar de [something or someone]:
	[PH-HI]to have an opinion about [something or someone]:[/PH-HI]
		¿Qué piensas del presidente?
		(What do you think about the president?)


pensar [infinitive]:
	[PH-HI]to plan on:[/PH-HI]
		Pensamos ir a la playa.
		(We're planning on going to the beach.)


no pensar que [prediction-subjunctive]:
	[PH-HI]to doubt that [prediction - with doubt] :[/PH-HI]
		No pienso que él salga.  (I doubt he's leaving.)


pensar que [prediction-indicative]
	[PH-HI]to think that [prediction- without doubt]:[/PH-HI]
		Pienso que mi hermano es rico. 
		(I think my brother is rich.)


pensar sobre [something]:
	[PH-HI]to think something over:[/PH-HI]
		Necesito pensar sobre la boda.  (I need to think over the wedding.)


pensar [something]:
	 [PH-HI]to think something up:[/PH-HI]
		¿Quién pensó este plan?  (Who dreamed up this plan?)


pensar que [prediction-subjunctive]:
	[PH-HI]to think that [prediction]:[/PH-HI]
		Pienso que vaya a llover.  (I think it is going to rain.)


dar que pensar a [someone]:
	[PH-HI]to give someone something to think about:[/PH-HI]
		Doy que pensar a mi hijo.  (I gave my son something to think about.)
