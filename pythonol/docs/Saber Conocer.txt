[PHTITLE]SABER VS. CONOCER


[PHSECTION]Use of 'Saber': 

[PH-HI]To indicate knowledge of facts or pieces of information.[/PH-HI]
	Ud. sabe su número de teléfono, ¿verdad?.  (You know your telephone number, right?)

[PH-HI]To know how to do something.[/PH-HI]
	¿Sabes jugar al ajedrez?  (Do you know how to play chess?)


[PHSECTION]Use of 'Conocer': 
  
[PH-HI]To know (be acquainted) with a person, place, or thing.[/PH-HI]
	Conozco un buen restaurante cerca de aquí. (I know a good restaurant near here.)

[PH-HI]To meet (a person or thing).[/PH-HI]
	¿Quieres conocer al nuevo profesor?  (Do you want to meet the new professor?)
