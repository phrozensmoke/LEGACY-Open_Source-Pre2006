[PHTITLE]THE PERSONAL 'A'

[PHSECTION]Rules for using the personal 'a': 
  
[PH-HI]When the direct object is a person or a loved animal use  verb + 'a' + direct object:[/PH-HI]
	Voy a invitar a mi amiga Patricia.  (I am going to invite my friend Patricia.)  
	Puedo llevar a Rufi, mi perro.  (I  could carry/bring Rufi, my dog.)


[PH-HI]Verbs are not followed by the personal 'a' if the direct object are unspecified people:[/PH-HI]
	Busco un hombre para esta película.    (I am looking for a man for this picture.)
	Busco a un hombre que vi ayer.  (I am looking for a man I saw yesterday.)


[PH-HI]'Tener' indicating posession is NOT followed with the personal 'a':[/PH-HI]
	Tengo una hermana.   (I have a sister.)
	Tengo a mi hermana en el hospital.  (I have a sister in the hospital.  - Expressing the 'existence' of a sister in  a certain state, not possession.)
